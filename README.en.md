## Three prompts, direct access (translated)

### The visibility test (the baseline question)

```text
Which [your trade] would you recommend in [your city], and why that one?
```

⚠️ The trap: if you ask your test questions inside the same conversation, your test is biased — once your brand appears in the thread, the AI keeps it in memory. The clean protocol (5 questions, 5 separate conversations, analysis prompt): [AI visibility test](https://www.david-corroy.com/prompts-geo/test-visibilite-ia/) (FR).

### The rushed-customer test

```text
Here is a web page: [paste your page's text]. Answer these 5 questions
using ONLY this page — if the information is not there, write
"THE PAGE DOES NOT SAY":
1) What does this company sell, precisely?
2) Where does it operate?
3) How much does it cost, even as a range?
4) Why this company rather than another?
5) How do I contact it, and how fast does it respond?
```

Every "THE PAGE DOES NOT SAY" is a gap that neither the AIs nor your customers will fill for you. [Full page →](https://www.david-corroy.com/prompts-geo/test-client-presse/) (FR)

### The single bio

```text
Here is my information, unsorted: [activity, founding date, figures, credentials, area].
PRODUCE: one reference bio of 50 to 80 words — factual, no superlatives — then
its variations: 1 sentence (directories), 30 words (social profiles), 120 words (about page).
RULE: each variation may say LESS than the reference bio, never SOMETHING ELSE.
```

One single story, told everywhere — the foundational brick of your entity. [Full page →](https://www.david-corroy.com/prompts-geo/bio-unique/) (FR)

## The shared anatomy of the 19 prompts

They all follow the same 6 rules ([the full guide, FR](https://www.david-corroy.com/prompts-geo/ecrire-un-prompt-fiable/)):

1. **One role, one mission** — not "help me": a precise job and a single task
2. **A required deliverable** — "PRODUCE EXACTLY:" locks the shape of the answer
3. **The anti-hallucination guardrail** — the AI writes `[TO BE PROVIDED]` instead of inventing
4. **Rule priority** — when instructions conflict, the order is defined upfront
5. **Self-checked quality control** — the prompt proofreads itself before handing in its work
6. **Zero filler** — no flattery, no generalities
